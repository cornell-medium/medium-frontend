Fostering Design at Cornell University

We believe design thinking is important in every field. Our mission is to teach the design process and inspire an appreciation of good design in all our participants. In doing so, we hope to facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration and strengthen Cornell's community as a whole. We fulfill our mission statement through a variety of design related workshops, talks, and expos. We also print a biannual design publication.
