Welcome

Fostering design at Cornell University

We believe the design process exists in every discipline. It is the blueprint that allows different disciplines to collaborate and innovate. We are Cornell University's first interdisciplinary design community and multi-dimensional platform that aims to connect disciplines through design. To accomplish this, we host design-focused events to bring together the Cornell community, publish an annual design review, and are working to build a web platform to improve the accessibility of design at Cornell.
