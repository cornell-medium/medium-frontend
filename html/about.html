What We Do

  • Bring together people interested in design at Cornell
    Our priority it to build a community by connecting and collaborating with existing organizations and disciplines through design. We were founded in response to a call from leaders across campus for a stronger student alliance towards deisgn.

  • Increase access to design tools and resources
    By uniting design organizations across campus, our objective is to make design tools and resouces easier to reach for those in need. The focus of our web platform is to provide a connection point and centralized resource to people interested in design.

  • Publish an annual design review
    The objective of our publication is to expose the diverse design pockets on campus and provide a unified perspective on Cornellâ€™s creative community. We typically publish in mid-spring of each year.

  • Host design events
    We host events for the Cornell community throughout the year to drive excitement about design on campus. Past events include the Raw Expo, Adobe workshops, and the Cornell Creative Sprint.

  • Provide design consulting
    As a center for the campus design community, we are always available to help connect people looking for help in their design pursuits with those who can help them. We pull knowledge from both our core team and the organizations we connect.

Our Mission

Using design in its different forms to inspire dialogue across disciplines. Our mission is to build an interdisciplinary community that promotes design education, culture and dialogue by connecting organizations and individuals through design. Our core values are:

  1. To increase dialogue amongst organizations about design on campus
  2. To connect design to other organizations and teams to build more innovative and holistic solutions
  3. To promote the value of design through interdisciplinary collaborations that have implications beyond academy into practice

Who We Are

A group of like-minded individuals. Medium's team originated as a group of students from Cornell's College of Architecture, Art, and Planning, and has since grown to incorporate students from many colleges and disciplines. Our team focuses on specific deliverables and projects to connect, create, and sustain the design community on campus. We accomplish this through four subteams:

Subteams

  • Events
    Plans and organizes all of the events coordinated by Medium, ranging from the Raw Expo to our annual design challenge.

  • Publication
    Works to publish our annual design review, following ideas from their inception to their realization on the page.

  • Brand
    Organizes our external presence and develops marketing materials for everything Medium.

  • Web
    Develops our web platform, built from the ground up using the Django web framework.
