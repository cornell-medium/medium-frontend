(Re)design Time
What is this?
You or someone you know has probably thought, “I wish I had more time!”
More time to sleep?
Another minute so you didn’t just miss that bus?
An extra few hours to complete that assignment?
Or perhaps a year to do... nothing.

(Re)design time is Medium's competition for this year. The style of competitions is open-ended: it could be physical artwork, writing, graphic design, or anything you can think of. We want to see your originality in relating your work to the concept of time and the process that brought you there.
