Events Team
The events team develops and hosts numerous workshops, panels, and talks throughout the semester, culminating in the Spring with the RAW Expo. Additionally, the events team hosts collaborative events with organizations throughout campus in order to further strengthen, and grow, the design community.
Design Team
The design team is in charge of developing a compelling brand around Medium. As a member of the design team, you will work with the Web, Editorial, and Events teams to iterate upon and ideate design solutions that further Medium’s mission.
Web Team
The web team is responsible for maintaining Medium’s site. As a member of the web team, you will learn how to build and deploy web apps in python, as well as become proficient with unix environments, version control, front end web development, and UI + UX design.
Editorial Team
The editorial team researches and authors articles concerning Cornell’s design community. As a member of the editorial team, you will work closely with the Web team in order to generate content for Medium’s digital editorial.
