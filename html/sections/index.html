mission
Medium Design Collective exists to connect independent thinkers of varying academic backgrounds through the design process. At its core, the design process is about breaking large problems into manageable ones — a skill that is valued in professionals of all fields. Throughout the year, Medium hosts a number of talks, expos, and workshops which serve the double purpose of teaching design and strengthening Cornell's community.
community
The members of Medium are very diverse in terms of both their academic interests and personal backgrounds.
