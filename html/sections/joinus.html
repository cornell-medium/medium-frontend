events team
The events team develops and hosts numerous workshops, panels, and talks throughout the semester, culminating with our annual exposition of creative processes, RAW EXPO, in the spring. Additionally, our team works to collaborate on events with other organizations across campus, in line with Medium's mission to further strengthen and expand Cornell's design community.
design team
The design team is in charge of developing a compelling brand around Medium. Members of the design team work with the web, editorial, and events teams to iterate upon and ideate design solutions that further Medium’s mission.
web team
The web team is responsible for maintaining Medium’s site. Members of the web team learn how to build and deploy web apps in Python, as well as become proficient with Unix environments, version control, front end web development, and UI + UX design.
editorial team
The editorial team researches and authors articles concerning Cornell’s design community. Members of the publication team work closely with the web team in order to generate content for Medium’s digital publication (new for 2017-18!).
