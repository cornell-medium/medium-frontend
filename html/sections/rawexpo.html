what is raw expo?
RAW EXPO is an exposition of the creative process across campus. Student groups from any of Cornell's colleges are free to show off their work. RAW puts an emphansis on works in progress, so projects in the process of iteration are ideal. RAW provides an environment for the public to give feedback on projects, and participating organizations gain visibility within Cornell's design community.
